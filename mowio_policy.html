Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

📋 Overview

Mowio ("the App") is a children's educational application that helps kids learn animal names and sounds in multiple languages. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

This policy explains that we collect absolutely no personal data or any other user data.

🔒 Data Collection

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any data about you or your device. Specifically, we never collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
  • Location data of any kind
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports
  • Photos, microphone input, or any media from the device
  • Cookies or any local tracking data

🛡️ Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, crash-reporting, or social-media SDKs. No data is shared with or sold to any third party under any circumstances.

🌐 Network Requests

The App connects to a server exclusively to download educational content — card images, animal sounds, and word labels. These requests contain no user information whatsoever.

No session tokens, identifiers, or IP-address logs are stored on the server.

👶 Children's Privacy

Mowio is designed for young children. Because no data of any kind is collected, the App is fully compliant with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • GDPR and GDPR-K (EU children's privacy provisions)
  • Google Play Families Policy requirements

No parental consent is required because no personal information is ever collected.

📱 App Permissions

The App requests only the permissions strictly necessary for its function:

  • Internet — to download card content from the server

No camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or location permissions are requested.

🗂️ Data Retention

Since no data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete. Uninstalling the App removes all locally cached content from your device.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is ever updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
